Who We Are

At Government College Of Engineering , Aurangabad (GECA) Alumni Network, we take immense pride in our illustrious history and the extraordinary individuals who have walked through our campus gates. Our alumni network is a testament to the profound impact of education and the lasting bonds formed during our college years. We invite you to be a part of this extraordinary journey as we celebrate our shared past and look forward to a bright future.our mission is to foster a vibrant and enduring community of graduates who are committed to strengthening the bonds that tie us to our beloved institution. We are dedicated to empowering our alumni to thrive in their personal and professional lives, to engage meaningfully with each other and their alma mater, and to collectively make a positive impact on society.

* To provide an organization through which the alumni of the Institute can keep in touch with the Students, faculty, and staff of the Institute, the activities of the Institute and with one another. To enable the institute to help in the different assistance required by the industry. * To take up sponsored projects in the various faculties and increase Industry Institute Interaction. * To provide testing and consultancy to the Alumni of the college in different fields like Civil, Structures, Environmental Engg, Computers, etc. * To enable the alumni to take part in such activities of the Institute and may contribute to the general improvement of the status of the Institute and alumni. * To help the alumni to get information from the Institute on various technical problems that they come across in their work. * To raise funds for creating various facilities at the institute. To enable alumni to help the Training and Placement of the students of this institute and to provide required manpower if they wish to make use of. * To arrange any activity for the benefit of the students, alumni and the faculty of the institute. * To Institute prizes and scholarships for students of the Institute on the basis of merit and render financial aid to deserving students, studying at the Institute To bring out a Bulletin periodically, highlighting the activities of the Institute and its alumni. * To further such other items as the Governing Body May decide from time to time in the interest of alumni of students or the faculty of the institute or the society.

* Provide conducive environment for academic in engineering education. * Enhance research and development along with promotion to sponsored projects and industrial consultancy. * Foster develpoment of students by creating awareness for needs of society, sustainable develpoment and human values.

In pursuit of global competitiveness, the institute is committed to excel in engineering education and research with concern for environment and society.
